Some Instructions

Box Selection

Simply click on a box to select it, hold and drag to place where desired. The Free Body Diagram will appear when a box has been selected. A box is deleted by dragging it off the side of the table.

Animating

To begin/pause animation, toggle the checkbox next to "ANIMATE". The passage of time can be increased or decreased by clicking the '+' or '-' buttons above the timer.

Forces

Forces may be added or deleted by clicking the appropriate button. The |F| slider controls the magnitude of the selected force. Clicking and dragging on the corresponding arrow on the main canvas allows orientation of the force. A new force is selected by clicking on the arrow.

No Presets are currently available.
